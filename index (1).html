Introduction

Hi, my name is Tiernon, and I’m currently a student preparing to study mechanical engineering at Texarkana after graduation. Throughout this semester in ENG 102, I’ve grown significantly as a writer—particularly in how I research and find credible sources to support my arguments. At first, I mostly summarized sources and struggled to connect them. Over time, I learned how to synthesize research, adapt tone for different audiences, and use feedback to make my ideas clearer and stronger.

I’ve included major writing projects from the semester, including my Literary Analysis of Frankenstein, Sharing Knowledge Project on teen mental health and social media, Literature Review, and Annotated Bibliography.
